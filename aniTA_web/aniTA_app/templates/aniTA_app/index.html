About AniTA

AniTA is an intelligent teaching assistant platform designed to streamline the educational experience for both professors and students. Leveraging advanced AI technology, AniTA automates assignment grading while providing comprehensive course management features. Our platform uses AI to analyze student submissions against professor-provided rubrics, delivering consistent and detailed feedback in minutes. This allows educators to focus on what matters most: teaching and mentoring students.
